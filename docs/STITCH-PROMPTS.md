## DOMAIN NAME SUGGESTIONS

### Tier 1 — Premium Brandable (My Top Picks)

| # | Domain | Why It Works | Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **driftsleep.com** | "Drift" evokes falling asleep naturally. Short, memorable, premium feel. Not a keyword-stuffed EMD — it's a BRAND. Think "Calm" for meditation. | Brand-first |
| 2 | **sleepdrift.co** | Same concept, .co is clean and tech-forward. Cheaper if .com is taken. | Brand-first |
| 3 | **sleepwise.io** | "Wise" implies intelligence/AI/data. The .io signals tech product. Works for the calculator + wearable data angle. | Smart/tech |
| 4 | **getcyclecalc.com** | Actionable ("get"), descriptive, easy to say. | Descriptive |
| 5 | **slumbr.co** | Trendy dropped-vowel naming (like Tumblr, Flickr). Memorable, short, brandable. | Startup/trendy |

### Tier 2 — SEO-Friendly + Brandable Hybrids

| # | Domain | Why It Works | Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| 6 | **sleepycalc.com** | Playful, memorable, still has "sleep" + "calc" for SEO. Easy to type. | Friendly/approachable |
| 7 | **cyclecalc.sleep** | The .sleep TLD exists — perfect match. Short. Unique. | Category TLD |
| 8 | **mysleeplab.com** | "Lab" implies science, data, testing. Fits the wearable + AI angle. | Science/authority |
| 9 | **sleepstackapp.com** | "Stack" implies comprehensive toolkit. Signals it's more than a basic calculator. | Tech/product |
| 10 | **deepcyclecalc.com** | "Deep" is evocative (deep sleep + deep analysis). Differentiated from plain "sleep calculator". | Descriptive + evocative |

### Tier 3 — EMD / Keyword-Heavy Options (Higher SEO Value, Less Brandable)

| # | Domain | Why It Works | Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| 11 | **sleepcyclecalculator.com** | Exact-match for "sleep cycle calculator" (10K-30K monthly searches). Long but ranks well. | Pure EMD |
| 12 | **sleeptimecalculator.com** | Targets "sleep time calculator". Very direct. | Pure EMD |
| 13 | **sleepscorecalc.com** | "Score" implies the wearable/data features. Unique positioning. | Hybrid |

### My Recommendation

**Go with #1 (driftsleep.com) or #6 (sleepycalc.com).**

**driftsleep.com** is the premium play — it's a real brand name that can grow beyond just a calculator site. It sounds like a $10K site. It works for the free tools AND the Pro subscription product. You can build "Drift" as a brand identity (logo, colors, tone). It also sounds great in conversation: "Check Drift Sleep for your ideal bedtime."

**sleepycalc.com** is the practical play — it's immediately obvious what the site does, it's fun/approachable, it's short, and it still has SEO value with "sleep" and "calc" in the domain. Less premium but higher instant recognition.

If you want maximum SEO power AND brand: buy both **driftsleep.com** (primary brand) and **sleepcyclecalculator.com** (redirect to primary, pure SEO value).
